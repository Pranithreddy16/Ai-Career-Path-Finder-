Bridging the Gap Between Skills and Careers

Career Copilot exists because students have skills but lack clear guidance on how those skills map to real, in-demand job roles.

Why Students Struggle

Many students feel lost because they know they can code, design, or analyze data but cannot see which roles actually match them.

Traditional counseling relies on long static tests and generic recommendations that ignore portfolios, projects, and behavior.

As a result, students waste semesters on misaligned paths, lose confidence, and struggle with employability.

Our Approach

Behavior-Aware

We use interactive micro-challenges to understand how you actually think and solve problems, not just what you click on a quiz.

Skill-to-Role Graph

A knowledge graph connects skills, roles, tools, and industries, making recommendations transparent and explainable.

Living Roadmaps

Your roadmap updates as you complete projects, courses, or internships, so guidance grows with you.
